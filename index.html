Our Journey to Forever

Our Story

Every great love story has a beginning. Ours started on campus, where a simple friendship slowly grew into a love story written by God.

  • How We Met

    October 25, 2012

    We met in 2012, in the first year of our Bachelor Degree studies (Level 100) at the University of Ghana, where a simple friendship took root. We spent those early years building trust, sharing laughter, and creating memories without realizing we were laying the groundwork for something greater.

  • From Friendship to Love

    Level 300 – University of Ghana

    What seemed like ordinary campus days were part of God's beautiful plan for our lives. By Level 300, our friendship naturally and gently turned into love. From that moment on, choosing each other felt effortless and right.

  • Our Traditional Marriage

    June 20, 2020

    On June 20, 2020, we were joined in a traditional marriage ceremony. Supported by family, we honored our roots and our love. It was simple, meaningful, and filled with joy. Two families became one. Forever began that day.
